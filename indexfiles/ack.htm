らくらくリスニング

〜MCJLE CD-ROM Audio Databank for Senior Students of Japanese 〜



This resource is based on the "Listening Tasks for Senior Secondary School Students of Japanese" published by the Melbourne Centre for Japanese Language Education in 2001.

This CD-ROM is designed for multipurpose teaching and learning. Learners can plan and practice their listening at their own pace. This resource
also allows teachers to integrate the use of computers into their curriculum ensuring that the ICT features in their course work, further developing useful and relevant skills.
　

This CD-ROM listening resource is designed for Years 11 and 12 students. Each task contains an audio file, questions, vocabulary list and sample
answers. Approximately 80 new 'preparation activities' have also been included in this resource, to address common linguistic elements that
can cause students difficulty.

This MCJLE CD-ROM Audio Databank for Senior Students of Japanese resource should only be used for teaching and learning purposes. Copyright restrictions apply to these materials. These materials may not be duplicated or used in any inappropriate way without the written permission of the Melbourne Centre for Japanese Language Education.

The らくらくリスニング may be placed on the school intranet for teaching and learning purposes but none of the materials may be placed in part or total on the internet .
