*Words listed above can be different word depending on by what kanji they are expressed in. For instance, しょくhas more than five different words such as 職, 色, 蝕, 初句, etc. This list contains words you do not need to memorize for senior school Japanese.
 
Step 1: Pronounce the above words paying attention to the little ゃ, ゅ and ょ marked in pink.
　
Step 2: Listen to the model pronunciation and repeat after the audio file. Each word will be repeated twice. Click the icon to start.
