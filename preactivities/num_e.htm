*general counter
The numbers above 10 are counted by こ. For example, 12 apples are りんご12こ.
 
*people in honorific form
You can make a honorific form to show your respect by adding お in front of 一人, 二人, and say お一人, お二人. While this expression used in both in daily and official situation, お一人様 and 一名(様) are used by the people in charge of customer service, such as waitress and hotel receptionist.
