Answers to listening exercise (c)

Japanese

1. 月曜日の夕方、じもとのクラブでテニスのれんしゅうをします。

English

I practice tennis at the local club on the Monday evenings.

Japanese

2. 毎朝、ニュースを見ながら朝ご飯を食べます。

English

I eat breakfast while watching the news every morning.

Japanese

3. 朝はバスで学校に行って、帰りは母が車でむかえにきてくれます。

English

I go to school by bus in the morning and my mother comes to pick me up in the afternoon (In Japanese, 帰り (on return) is used instead of 夕方は in this content.)

Japanese

4. たいてい夜七時半ごろ、家ぞくと晩ご飯を食べます。

English

I usually eat dinner at around 7.30 at night with my family.

Japanese

5. 火曜日と木曜日は、夕方5時から8時までじゅくがあります。

English

I have cram school from 5 in the evening to 8 on Tuesdays and Thursdays.

Japanese

6. 夜中に、母はおにぎりを作ってくれます。

English

My mother makes rice ball/onigiri in the middle of the night for me.

Japanese

7. あしたの午前中、ひまですか。

English

Will you be free tomorrow morning?

Japanese

8. ぶかつは、平日の朝と放課後(ほうかご)に毎日あります。

English

I have club activities in the morning and after school on weekdays every day.

Japanese

9. 日曜日の正午に駅の前で会いましょう。

English

Let’s meet in front of the station at noon on Sunday.

Japanese

10. 土曜の夜はよくカラオケに行きます。

English

I often go to karaoke on Saturday nights.
