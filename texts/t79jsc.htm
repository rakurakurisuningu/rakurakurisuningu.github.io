VOCABULARY 

AによるとBはadjective root/verb plain + そうです。

According to A, it seems that B + adjective/verb

レンタルビデオ屋

rental video shop

verb stem + はじめます

start to do ~

作られています

to have been produced (passive form)

売(う)ります

to sell

かります

to rent, borrow, hire

 

 

 

 

 

*Passive verb forms “〜れます” show the effect of the action that someone or something is receiving. It is usually translated as “to be done” in English.

Group 1 verbs: -imasu → -karemasu (e.g. 書かれます、読まれます)

Group 2 verbs: -masu → -raremasu (e.g.食たべられます、見られます)

Group 3 verbs: きます→こられます、します→されます
